Cricket

Cricket is sport were two teams of 11 people play. Batsmen who have to hit the ball with a wooden bat to score runs. Bowler tries to get the batsmen out. If the ball hits the wicket the batsman is out. Some of the other ways the batsman can get out is if fielder catches the ball hit by batsman before it hits the ground. Leg before wicket is when batsman's leg is infront of the wicket and the ball hits its an out. Rules fo balling your arm has to be straight and you cannot cross the crease line.
